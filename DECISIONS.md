# Batho Travels Decisions Log

This file records product, architecture, design, and policy decisions for Batho Travels. Any future substitution or judgment call should be added here with date, owner, decision, rationale, and implications.

## Decision Table

| Date | Area | Decision | Rationale | Implications |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2026-06-04 | Product | Use "Batho Travels" everywhere in user-facing copy. | The brief explicitly locks the platform name and avoids ambiguity. | App metadata, emails, SEO, ASO, legal copy, and UI must never shorten the name to "Batho" alone. |
| 2026-06-04 | Scope | Build Full V1 in phases, starting with sign-off documentation. | The approved plan targets the complete premium system, not a narrowed demo. | Product code waits until `ARCHITECTURE.md`, `DESIGN.md`, and this log are approved. |
| 2026-06-04 | Positioning | Treat Batho Travels as structured travel savings, not lending, banking, BNPL, or credit. | The product promise depends on trust, transparency, and non-predatory behavior. | No late fees, no loan language, no credit checks, no debt framing, no shame-based reminders. |
| 2026-06-04 | UX Direction | Use the Warm Calm premium design direction. | The user selected a grounded, human, refined travel/savings experience with strong trust cues. | Design uses warm neutrals, grounded primary color, confident accent, calm motion, and premium restraint. |
| 2026-06-04 | AI Planner | Make user-driven AI planning the center of V1. | The AI planner is the product moat and differentiator. | No prebuilt trip catalogue or package funnel in V1. Templates may be layered later only if architecture supports them. |
| 2026-06-04 | Funding | Use sequential staged funding: flights, then stay, then experiences. | Flights are time-sensitive and volatile, so they must fund first. | Calculator, dashboards, receipts, and admin views must never allocate every payment by a fixed percentage split. |
| 2026-06-04 | Plan Limits | Default to 12 months, max 12 months, configurable later. | The brief locks V1 to 3 to 12 months while allowing future expansion. | Put `MAX_PLAN_MONTHS` in shared config rather than scattering literals. |
| 2026-06-04 | Trip Minimums | Enforce domestic 3 months, Africa regional 6 months, long-haul international 12 months. | Longer trips require more realistic planning windows. | Calculator and AI planner must reject shorter plans with warm explanatory copy. |
| 2026-06-04 | Late Payment | Use 7-day grace, then pause, adjust, or cancel. | Batho Travels must avoid punitive debt-like mechanics. | No R150 fees, no escalating ladders, no penalty copy, no shame notifications. |
| 2026-06-04 | Cancellation | Use refund tiers with a flat R500 management fee. | The brief frames this as admin/ops cost, not punishment. | Fee and tiers live in config and appear clearly in cancellation flows. |
| 2026-06-04 | Reservation Copy | Use "Reserve your spot" and "Secure your travel plan early"; never "lock in". | Inventory is not confirmed until fully funded and booked with suppliers. | Copy reviews must flag over-promising inventory language. |
| 2026-06-04 | Payments | Implement Paystack, Stripe, and Ozow behind `PaymentProvider`. | Provider flexibility is required and raw card data must never touch Batho systems. | Marketing remains provider-agnostic, while backend stores only references and receipts. |
| 2026-06-04 | Backend | Use Convex for database, real-time sync, server functions, and scheduled jobs. | The brief recommends Convex for live dashboards and cron-like reminders. | Claude, payment verification, notification jobs, and reminders run server-side. |
| 2026-06-04 | Monorepo | Use pnpm workspaces and Turborepo. | Shared domain logic and design tokens must serve mobile, web, and admin. | Packages are split into UI, tokens, core, payments, and config. |
| 2026-06-04 | Typography | Recommend Fraunces for display and Source Sans 3 for body/UI. | This pairing supports warmth, readability, and a premium travel feel without generic AI defaults. | Load fonts consistently across web and mobile, with tabular number handling for money. |
| 2026-06-04 | Color | Use warm neutrals, grounded terracotta primary, teal accent, and restrained gold. | The palette supports trust, humanity, South African warmth, and travel energy without fintech aggression. | Tokens must define intentional light and dark themes with WCAG AA contrast checks. |
| 2026-06-04 | Admin UX | Separate operations, finance, and support workspaces by role. | Internal users need safe, focused queues with least-privilege access. | Admin navigation, routes, and data permissions must reflect role boundaries. |
| 2026-06-04 | SEO/GEO | Build structured pages, FAQ, JSON-LD, en-ZA locale, ZAR, sitemap, robots, and `llms.txt`. | Batho Travels must be understandable and citable by search engines and answer engines. | Landing content must include factual, self-contained statements and not hide key facts in client-only rendering. |
| 2026-06-04 | Foundation | Scaffold the monorepo before implementing core business logic. | The approved plan separates project foundation from domain behavior so each phase can be reviewed cleanly. | Phase 2 creates apps, packages, tokens, schemas, and setup files. Calculator, AI planner, real payments, and production auth come in later phases. |
| 2026-06-04 | Core Domain | Implement pure TypeScript policy functions in `@batho/core`. | Savings, refund, and KYC behavior must be deterministic and shared by mobile, web, admin, and backend. | `calculateSavingsPlan`, `allocateStagedFunding`, `validateTripPlanLength`, `calculateCancellationRefund`, and `canBookAmount` now encode the V1 policy rules with unit tests. |
| 2026-06-04 | AI Planner | Put planner contracts, seasonality seed data, and validation in `@batho/core`; keep Claude calls server-side in Convex. | The planner is the product moat, but outputs must stay deterministic, validated, and grounded in destination data. | `validatePlannerResult`, `createDraftPlannerResult`, value-month recommendations, Convex planner persistence, and the mobile planner screen now form the phase 4 foundation. |
| 2026-06-04 | Trips Dashboard | Build dashboard state from the same staged-funding core used by the calculator. | The mobile app, web app, admin, and backend must agree on saved amounts, remaining amounts, active stage, next contribution, and payment history. | `createTripFromPlannerResult`, `buildTripDashboard`, and `recordTripPayment` now power the mobile dashboard preview and Convex trip creation/query functions. |
| 2026-06-04 | Payment Adapters | Keep Paystack, Stripe, and Ozow behind one tokenized provider boundary. | Batho Travels must support trusted providers without storing raw card or bank details or making the UI provider-dependent. | `@batho/payments` now normalizes intent creation, verification, refunds, receipts, and statuses; Convex payment actions create pending payments and refresh staged funding after confirmed payments. |
| 2026-06-05 | KYC | Treat KYC as protective verification, not credit assessment. | The product must stay debt-free and non-lender-like while still protecting higher-value bookings and compliance review. | `@batho/core` defines Standard and Enhanced verification requirements, mobile has a verification flow, admin has a KYC review workspace, and Convex has submission/review mutations with audit logs. |
| 2026-06-05 | Notifications | Use reminders as supportive plan care, not collections. | Batho Travels must keep travellers informed without shame, penalties, or lender-like escalation. | `@batho/core` now classifies due-soon, grace, and support-review timing; mobile has reminder preferences and support options; Convex jobs queue reminders, open support review after grace, and retry failed notifications. |
| 2026-06-05 | Groups And Custom Destinations | Group trips use independent participant shares, and custom destinations require operations review. | V1 must support shared travel planning without making one traveller responsible for another person's savings, and custom destinations must not become a package catalogue. | `@batho/core` validates group shares and destination request quality; mobile has a groups/custom destination screen; Convex has group trip and custom destination workflows; admin has an operations review queue. |
| 2026-06-05 | Admin Panel | Keep operations, finance, and support workspaces role-separated while sharing audit visibility. | Internal users need dense, scannable queues without crossing responsibilities or weakening traceability. | `@batho/core` defines admin workspace access and queue summaries; admin now shows operations, finance, support, KYC, users, and audit surfaces; Convex exposes admin dashboard and recent audit queries. |
| 2026-06-05 | Landing And Discovery | Make the public landing page a premium AI-planner-first travel surface with SEO, GEO, and ASO assets. | Search engines, answer engines, and app stores need clear, factual statements about Batho Travels without confusing it for lending or packaged trips. | Web now has full landing content, JSON-LD, en-ZA and ZAR metadata, sitemap, robots, `llms.txt`, machine-readable GEO facts, and app-store copy. |
| 2026-06-05 | QA And Launch Prep | Treat Phase 12 as a launch gate with reusable seed data, automated checks, and explicit external sign-off. | The V1 needs a repeatable release process that separates local verification from production credentials, legal review, stores, and real-device testing. | `QA.md`, `LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md`, launch seed data, and `phase12:check` scripts define the final hardening path. |
