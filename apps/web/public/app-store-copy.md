# Batho Travels App Store Copy

## App Name

Batho Travels

## Subtitle

AI trip planning with zero-debt savings

## Short Description

Plan custom trips with AI, save monthly in ZAR, and travel with 0% interest, no credit checks, no loans, and no debt.

## Long Description

Batho Travels helps South African travellers plan meaningful trips early and save for them calmly over time.

Start with the AI Trip Planner. Share your monthly budget, interests, trip type, and travel season, then review destination suggestions with estimated costs, itinerary ideas, and clear assumptions. When a plan feels right, Batho Travels hands it into a structured savings plan without duplicate entry.

Savings are simple and transparent. Flights are funded first, then stay, then experiences. Plans use 0% interest, no credit checks, no loans, and no debt. If life changes, support flows are built around grace, pause, adjust, or cancel.

Batho Travels also supports group trip coordination and custom destination requests. Each traveller can keep an independent savings plan while the group stays aligned around dates, progress, and readiness.
